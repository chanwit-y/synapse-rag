# Epic 9 — Analytics & Users (Planned)

> **Status: Planned / Backlog.** As of this writing the `/analytics` and
> `/users` routes are placeholder "coming soon" pages and are not yet in the
> primary navigation. These stories capture the intended direction, not current
> behavior.

---

## US-9.1 — Usage analytics dashboard *(Planned)*

**As an** Administrator, **I want** a dashboard of platform usage,
**so that** I can understand how documents, RAGs, and chat are being used.

**Acceptance criteria (proposed)**
- Show high-level counts (collections, documents, RAGs, chats).
- Show activity over time (e.g. saves, imports, queries).
- Respect the active light/dark theme.

---

## US-9.2 — User management *(Planned)*

**As an** Administrator, **I want** to manage users and their access,
**so that** I can control who can read and edit content.

**Acceptance criteria (proposed)**
- List users with their roles.
- Invite/create, edit, and deactivate users.
- Map users to the personas/roles referenced across these stories
  (Author, Researcher, Administrator, Integrator).
